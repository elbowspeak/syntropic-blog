## Infrastructure Assessment

Grid-scale battery storage is entering a period of genuine mass deployment, not just capacity announcements. The U.S. pipeline of 24 GW for 2026 — against a baseline of 15 GW delivered in 2025 — is ambitious but grounded in a development pipeline that was largely in advanced stages entering the year. Global BESS shipments growing 75.5% year-over-year to 421 GWh in 2025, with 600 GWh projected for 2026, confirms that manufacturing supply chains are now operating at industrial scale.

**The primary bottlenecks are not technical — they are systemic:**

1. **Interconnection queues**: Despite manufacturing capacity expanding rapidly, grid connection timelines remain a significant drag on commissioned capacity. Projects like the Rolls-Royce/Voltaria Scotland facility (grid connection 2026, online 2027) and the Vena Energy Tailem Bend 3 project (targeted 2027) illustrate the lag between project announcement and operational status.

2. **Transmission permitting**: The 12–17 year average timeline for new transmission approvals in Europe (and comparable friction in the U.S.) means that even well-funded projects face structural delays. Near-term infrastructure upgrades to existing mid- and high-voltage lines are emerging as the more viable path.

3. **Critical mineral price volatility**: The 78% lithium carbonate price spike is a real cost shock to projects in development. Projects with fixed EPC contracts signed before December 2025 are particularly exposed. This will likely cause selective delays in lithium-ion projects while accelerating procurement interest in alternative chemistries.

**Momentum shifts to watch:**
- Sodium-ion is transitioning from pilot to product on a faster-than-expected timeline. The MISO deployment and CATL's Naxtra commercial launch represent a phase change.
- Long-duration storage is being pulled into utility IRPs by coal retirement timelines — a demand signal that is structural, not cyclical.
- VPPs are crossing into regulatory mandate territory, which will compress the timeline for large-scale deployment.
