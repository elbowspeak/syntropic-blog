IRA domestic content ramp requirements intensify through 2029, creating compliance risk for projects that cannot source domestically.

**Momentum shifts** worth flagging: The AI data center demand signal is becoming the clearest structural demand driver for both LDES and HVDC investment that was not present in planning assumptions 18 months ago.
