**Late-Ordering Fabs / Capacity Constrained Players**
With equipment lead times poised to extend and 67,000 jobs unfilled by 2030, any fab without confirmed equipment orders and workforce pipelines faces meaningful execution risk on announced capacity plans. Second-tier foundries are most exposed.
