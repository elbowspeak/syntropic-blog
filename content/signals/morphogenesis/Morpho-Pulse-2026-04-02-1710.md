The deepest cross-pollination is conceptual: the framing of morphogenesis as **goal-directed computation** is beginning to influence how AI researchers think about embodied agents and developmental learning. The reverse flow — AI concepts like reinforcement learning being imported to describe planarian regeneration — is equally active. This bidirectional exchange is a hallmark of fields approaching a paradigm shift.
