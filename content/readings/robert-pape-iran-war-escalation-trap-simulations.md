A masterclass in how optimizing for precision on the wrong level of analysis produces catastrophe. Pape's core insight: regimes are matrices, not Jenga towers. Taking out a node doesn't collapse an adaptive system — it reorganizes, usually more aggressively. The Supreme Leader killed had issued two fatwas against nuclear weapons; his successor has issued none. Iran models American politics better than America models Iran — the asymmetric advantage of playing the long game.
