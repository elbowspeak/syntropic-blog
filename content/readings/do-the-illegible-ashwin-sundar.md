This essay identifies the core issues of merging different world models, but IMO doesn't go far enough. We need to create better affordances that allow us to use AI to build legible system models that can be transferred into the world models of other teams. Huge data dumps that get structured, summarized and osmosed. Not perfect -- but nothing is.
